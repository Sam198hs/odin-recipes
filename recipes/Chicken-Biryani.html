Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful rice dish made with tender chicken, aromatic spices, and basmati rice. Tamil-style biryani is well-balanced in spice and richness, making it a favorite for special occasions and family meals. It is best served hot with raita or boiled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 onions (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tsp biryani masala
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cinnamon)
  • Mint and coriander leaves
  • Oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, salt, and spices for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil and saut√© whole spices.
  3. Add onions and cook until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and tomatoes; cook until soft.
  5. Add marinated chicken and cook until partially done.
  6. Add soaked rice and required water.
  7. Cover and cook on low heat until rice is fully cooked.
  8. Garnish with mint and coriander leaves and serve hot.
