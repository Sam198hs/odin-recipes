Peper Chicken

Pepper Chicken is a simple yet flavorful dish made with freshly crushed black pepper and aromatic spices. This semi-dry chicken recipe is known for its spicy kick and comforting taste. It is perfect as a side dish with rice or chapati.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken
  • 2 onions (sliced)
  • 1 tbsp crushed black pepper
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil and saut√© onions until soft.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook well.
  3. Add chicken, turmeric, and salt.
  4. Cook covered until chicken is fully done.
  5. Add crushed black pepper and curry leaves.
  6. Cook until dry and aromatic.
  7. Serve hot with rice or chapati.
