Chicken Chettinad

Chicken Chettinad is a bold and spicy South Indian dish from Tamil Nadu. It is known for its rich blend of freshly ground spices, aromatic curry leaves, and deep flavors. This dish pairs perfectly with rice, dosa, or parotta and is loved for its intense and authentic taste.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken
  • 2 onions (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp Chettinad masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • Curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until raw smell disappears.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Add spices and mix well.
  5. Add chicken pieces and stir to coat with masala.
  6. Cover and cook until chicken is tender.
  7. Simmer until thick gravy forms and serve hot.
